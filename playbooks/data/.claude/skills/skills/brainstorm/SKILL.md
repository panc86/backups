Challenge me relentlessly questioning every aspect of a new feature until we reach a mutual understanding.
Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.
For each of my answers, provide **tradeoffs**, **counter-arguments** and alternatives, if applicable.
If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
